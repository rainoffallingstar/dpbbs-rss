Decompensated metabolic acidosis in the emergency department: Epidemiology, sodium bicarbonate therapy, and clinical outcomes

CONCLUSIONS: ABG-confirmed decompensated metabolic acidosis was rare but associated with a high mortality. SB administration occurred in a minority of patients and in more acidaemic patients. However, SB dose was stereotypical and not tailored to acidosis severity. Assessment of SB effect was infrequent and showed no correction of acidosis.
