Post-transplant Recurrence of Focal Segmental Glomerulosclerosis: Consensus Statements

Focal segmental glomerulosclerosis (FSGS) is one of the primary causes of nephrotic syndrome in both pediatric and adult patients, which can lead to end-stage kidney disease. Recurrence of FSGS after kidney transplantation significantly increases allograft loss, leading to morbidity and mortality.
