Preclinical efficacy of carfilzomib in BRAF-mutant colorectal cancer models

We have previously reported that BRAF-mutant human cells display a high rate of protein production, causing proteotoxic stress, and are selectively sensitive to the proteasome inhibitors bortezomib and carfilzomib.
