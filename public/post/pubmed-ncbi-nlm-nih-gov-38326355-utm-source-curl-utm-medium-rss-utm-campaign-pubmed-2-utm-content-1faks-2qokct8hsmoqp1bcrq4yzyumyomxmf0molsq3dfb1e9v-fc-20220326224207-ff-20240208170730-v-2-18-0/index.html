A convolutional neural network to identify mosquito species (Diptera: Culicidae) of the genus Aedes by wing images

Accurate species identification is crucial to assess the medical relevance of a mosquito specimen, but requires intensive experience of the observers and well-equipped laboratories. In this proof-of-concept study, we developed a convolutional neural network (CNN) to identify seven Aedes species by wing images, only.
