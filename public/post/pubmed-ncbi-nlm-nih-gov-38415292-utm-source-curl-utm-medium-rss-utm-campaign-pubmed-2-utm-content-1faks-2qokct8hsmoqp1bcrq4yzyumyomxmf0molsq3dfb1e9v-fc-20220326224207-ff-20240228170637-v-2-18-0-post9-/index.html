Inhibition of 26S proteasome activity by α-synuclein is mediated by the proteasomal chaperone Rpn14/PAAF1

Parkinson’s disease (PD) is characterized by aggregation of α-synuclein (α-syn) into protein inclusions in degenerating brains. Increasing amounts of aggregated α-syn species indicate significant perturbation of cellular proteostasis. Altered proteostasis depends on α-syn protein levels and the impact of α-syn on other components of the proteostasis network.
