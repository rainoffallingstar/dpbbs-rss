Macrophage Mediated Abscopal Effects of Radiation Therapy

CONCLUSION: Our data clearly demonstrate macrophage-mediated abscopal responses of RT when combined with CD47 blockade in a range of cancer models. The systemic activation of antitumor macrophages following radiation and CD47 blockade may be particularly important in cancer patients who suffer from metastatic disease.
