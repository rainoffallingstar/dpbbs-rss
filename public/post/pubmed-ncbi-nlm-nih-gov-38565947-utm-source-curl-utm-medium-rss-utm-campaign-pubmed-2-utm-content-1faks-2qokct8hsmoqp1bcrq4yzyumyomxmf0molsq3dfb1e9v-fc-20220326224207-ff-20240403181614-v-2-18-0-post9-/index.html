ARGONAUTE10 controls cell fate specification and formative cell divisions in the Arabidopsis root

In the root vasculature, a gradient of miRNA165/6 controls the abundance of HD-ZIP III transcription factors, which in turn control cell fate and spatially restrict vascular cell proliferation to specific cells.
