Adding liposomal doxorubicin enhances the abscopal effect induced by radiation/αPD1 therapy depending on tumor cell mitochondrial DNA and cGAS/STING

CONCLUSIONS: These data show that single low-dose Doxil can substantially enhance the RT/αPD-1-induced abscopal effect, with a strong increase in cross-presenting DCs and CD8^(+) tumor-specific T cells particularly in abscopal tumors compared with RT/αPD-1 and Doxil/αPD-1. Moreover, they indicate that the mtDNA/cGAS/STING/IFN-I axis is important for the immunogenic/immunomodulatory doxorubicin effects. Our ﬁndings may be helpful for the planning of clinical radiochemoimmunotherapy trials …
