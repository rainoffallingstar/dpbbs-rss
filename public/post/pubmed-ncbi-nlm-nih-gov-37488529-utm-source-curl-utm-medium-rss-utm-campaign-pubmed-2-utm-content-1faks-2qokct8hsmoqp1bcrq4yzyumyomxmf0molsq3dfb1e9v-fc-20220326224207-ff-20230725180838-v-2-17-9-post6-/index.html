A network medicine approach to study comorbidities in heart failure with preserved ejection fraction

CONCLUSIONS: We applied systems medicine concepts to analyze comorbidity profiles in a HF patient cohort. We were able to identify disease clusters that helped to characterize HF patients. We derived a distinct comorbidity profile for HFpEF, which was leveraged to suggest novel candidate genes via network propagation.
