Psilocybin does not induce the vulnerability marker HSP70 in neurons susceptible to Olney's lesions

Both compounds increase glutamate signalling and evoke cortical hyperexcitation. S-ketamine induces neurotoxicity especially in the retrosplenial cortex (Olney’s lesions).
