Disulfide bridge-dependent dimerization triggers FGF2 membrane translocation into the extracellular space

This process is initiated by phosphatidylinositol-4,5-bisphosphate (PI(4,5)P(2))-dependent formation of highly dynamic FGF2 oligomers at the inner plasma membrane leaflet, inducing the formation of lipidic membrane pores.
