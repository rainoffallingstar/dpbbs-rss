Comprehensive identification of diverse ribosomal RNA modifications by targeted nanopore direct RNA sequencing and JACUSA2

Ribosomal RNAs are decorated by numerous post-transcriptional modifications whose exact roles in ribosome biogenesis, function, and human pathophysiology remain largely unknown. Here, we report a targeted direct rRNA sequencing approach involving a substrate selection step and demonstrate its suitability to identify differential modification sites in combination with the JACUSA2 software.
