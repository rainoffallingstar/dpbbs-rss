DOAC plasma concentration upon hospital admission in a cohort of trauma patients. An observational real-life study

CONCLUSION: The current study revealed that patients on dabigatran and rivaroxaban were prone to higher DOAC plasma levels upon hospital admission in comparison with apixaban and edoxaban. DOAC plasma level measurement in trauma patients might be warranted due to unpredictively low or high plasma concentrations.
