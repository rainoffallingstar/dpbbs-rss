Mst4, a novel cardiac STRIPAK complex associated kinase, regulates cardiomyocyte growth and survival and is upregulated in human cardiomyopathy

Myocardial failure is associated with adverse remodeling which includes apoptotic loss of cardiomyocytes, hypertrophy as well as alterations in cell-cell contacts. Striatin-interacting phosphatase and kinase (STRIPAK) complexes and their kinase Mst4 have been linked to the development of different diseases.
