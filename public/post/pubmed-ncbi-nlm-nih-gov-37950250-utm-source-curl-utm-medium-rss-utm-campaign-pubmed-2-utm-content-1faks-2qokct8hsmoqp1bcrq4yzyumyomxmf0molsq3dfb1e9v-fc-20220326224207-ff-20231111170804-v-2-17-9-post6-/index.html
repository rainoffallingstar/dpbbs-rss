Measurement invariance and adapted preferences: evidence for the ICECAP-A and WeRFree instruments

CONCLUSIONS: This study tests the measurement invariance properties of the WeRFree and ICECAP-A instruments. The results indicate that these instruments were scalar invariant in all subsamples, which means that group means can be compared across different subpopulations.
