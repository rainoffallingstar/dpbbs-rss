AutophagyNet: High-resolution data source for the analysis of autophagy and its regulation

Macroautophagy/autophagy is a highly-conserved catabolic process eliminating dysfunctional cellular components and invading pathogens. Autophagy malfunction contributes to disorders such as cancer, neurodegenerative and inflammatory diseases. Understanding autophagy regulation in health and disease has been the focus of the last decades.
