Graphene nanoribbons are one-dimensional stripes of graphene with width- and edge-structure-dependent electronic properties. Here we investigate the optical properties of solution-synthesized 9-armchair graphene nanoribbons (9-aGNRs) that are stabilized as dispersions in organic solvents and further fractionated by liquid cascade centrifugation (LCC).
