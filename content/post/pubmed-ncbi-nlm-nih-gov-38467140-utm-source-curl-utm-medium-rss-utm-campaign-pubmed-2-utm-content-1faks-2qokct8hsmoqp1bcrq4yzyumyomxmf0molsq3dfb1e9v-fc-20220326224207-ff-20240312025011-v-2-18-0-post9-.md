The LFK index has been promoted as an improved method to detect bias in meta-analysis. Putatively, its performance does not depend on the number of studies in the meta-analysis. We conducted a simulation study, comparing the LFK index test to three standard tests for funnel plot asymmetry in settings with smaller or larger group sample sizes.
