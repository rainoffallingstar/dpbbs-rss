Epithelioid hemangioma is a benign vascular neoplasm with a characteristic histological and immunohistochemical pattern, characterized by a lymphocytic inflammatory infiltrate with admixed eosinophils and FOS‑B expression. The correct diagnosis is of particular relevance, since malignant vascular tumors with differentiated epithelioid cells can also be considered in the differential diagnosis.
