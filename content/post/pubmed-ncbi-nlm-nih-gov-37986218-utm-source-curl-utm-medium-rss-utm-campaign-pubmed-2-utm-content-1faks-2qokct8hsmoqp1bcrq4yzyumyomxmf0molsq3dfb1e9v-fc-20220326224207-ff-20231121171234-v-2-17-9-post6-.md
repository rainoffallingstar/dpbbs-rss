The incorporation of non-canonical amino acids (ncAAs) into proteins is a powerful technique used in various research fields. Genetic code expansion (GCE) is the most common way to achieve this: a specific codon is selected to be decoded by a dedicated tRNA orthogonal to the endogenous ones.
