Identifying potential cancer-associated genes and drug targets from omics data is challenging due to its diverse sources and analyses, requiring advanced skills and large amounts of time. To facilitate such analysis, we developed Cat-E (Cancer Target Explorer), a novel R/Shiny web tool designed for comprehensive analysis with evaluation according to cancer-related omics data.
