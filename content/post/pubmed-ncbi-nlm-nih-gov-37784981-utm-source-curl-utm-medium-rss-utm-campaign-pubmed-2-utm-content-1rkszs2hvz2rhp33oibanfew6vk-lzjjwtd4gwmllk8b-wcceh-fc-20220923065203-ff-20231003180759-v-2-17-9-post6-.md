CONCLUSION: Our data clearly demonstrate macrophage-mediated abscopal responses of RT when combined with CD47 blockade in a range of cancer models. The systemic activation of antitumor macrophages following radiation and CD47 blockade may be particularly important in cancer patients who suffer from metastatic disease. RT is a part of standard-of-care and CD47-blocking strategies are in clinical trials, therefore our observations may be rapidly translatable to cancer ...
